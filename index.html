Discover Taniti

Photo by Maarten van den Heuvel

You are invited to explore the enchanted island of Taniti!

Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific with an area of less than 500 square miles. The terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, and the island is home to a small but safe harbor. There is so much to discover here-from the the lush tropical rainforests, to the mountainous interior that even includes a small, active volcano!
