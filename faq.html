Taniti-FAQ and Contact

Photo by Maarten van den Heuvel

We are here to help!

Below is a list of frequently asked questions. If you have a question that is not answered here, or would like to make a reservation, please contact us using the form at the bottom of the page.



What is the population of Taniti?

Taniti has an indigenous population of aboout 20,000 people.


What is the economy of Taniti?

The economy of Taniti is primarily based on tourism, agriculture, and fishing. Until recently, the Tanitian economy was dominated by finishing and agriculture. Now, thanks to the natural beautry of the island, tourism has become a major economic driver.


Can I use a normal electrical plug?

Power outlets are 120 volts (The same as in the United States).


Is alcohol available on the island?

Yes, there are several bars and restaurants on the island that serve alcohol. However, alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 AM.


What is the drinking age on Taniti?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18, however the drinking age is not strictly enforced


Is English spoken on the island?

Yes, English is widely spoken by many younger Tanitians. However, very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents.


Is there a hospital on the island?

Yes, there is a hospital on the island as well as several clinics. The hospital has many multilingual employees.


Is the island safe? Is there violent crime?

Violent crime is very rare on Taniti, but as tourism increases, there ar emore reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.


Are there national holidays on the island of Taniti?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holidays, and many tourist attracdtions and restaurants will be closed on holidays, so visitors should plan accordingly.


Is there a local currency on the island?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency excahnge, and many businesses accept major credit cards.


Is it easy to walk around Taniti?

Yes, the island is relatively flat and has many walkable areas. Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing because it is easy to explore on foot.


Is it easy to bike around Taniti?

Yes, there are several bike rental options available on the island. Bikes and helmets are avilable for rent, and helmets are required by law. The terrain is generally flat, making it easy to ride around.
