Taniti-Activities

Photo by Maarten van den Heuvel

There are so many activities to enjoy!

Whether you want to relax on the beach, explore the island, or have an adventure, Taniti has something for everyone. We have a variety of activities to suit your interests and preferences. No matter what you choose to do, you're sure to have an unforgettable experience on the enchanting island of Taniti!



Many activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. Tourists alsso enjoy sightseeing in Taniti City, which boasts native architecture and is near the white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.
