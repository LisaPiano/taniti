Taniti-Transportation

Photo by Maarten van den Heuvel

It is easy to get to and around the beautiful island of Taniti!

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive by cruise ship.


Taniti Airport

Airplane icon by Icons8

Taniti International Airport is a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Reserve a Cruise Ship

Cruise Ship icon by Icons8

We can help you reserve a cruise that you can travel to Taniti by sea. A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.
